Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to high speed blender
    1. Turn the knob to the high setting!
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
    4. If you let it sit for more than 5 days it will go BAD
